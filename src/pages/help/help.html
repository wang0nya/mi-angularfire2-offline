Register a supplier

The first thing you'll need to do is register a supplier. You'll not be able to add stock without this.

Register a supplier

You can edit your supplier details and also call or email them through the details you registered. How cool is that? :).

Add Stocks

After you've registered some suppliers, you can now add some stock from the Stocks page.

Stocks

Your stocks reflect the items you usually deal with, who supplies them and other details, not necessarily what you have in your store right now.

Buy

To add items to your store, buy from your stocks. Click on an item in your stocks page, it'll give you an option to buy.

Buy some more

To buy some more of the same item, click on it from the purchases page and choose 'Add Stock'.

Sell

To register a sale, click on an item in your purchases and click sell from the options.

Sell some more

To sell more of the same item, click on it from the sales page and choose 'Add Sale'.
